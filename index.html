Top 10 Destinations in Zamboanga

Explore the most beautiful and culturally rich spots in the City of Flowers

Discover Zamboanga

Unforgettable Adventures Await

From pristine pink sand beaches to historic Spanish forts, Zamboanga offers a unique blend of natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Let our expert guides show you the best of what this beautiful city has to offer.

Book a Tour Now

Great Santa Cruz Island

Famous for its unique pink sand beach, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life. Perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and beach relaxation.

01

Fort Pilar

Historic Spanish-era fortress and shrine, showcasing Zamboanga's rich colonial heritage and religious significance with stunning architecture.

02

Pasonanca Park

A natural tree park featuring swimming pools, picnic areas, and lush greenery. Ideal for family outings and nature lovers.

03

Paseo del Mar

Beautiful waterfront promenade offering breathtaking sunset views, local food stalls, and a perfect spot for evening walks.

04

Yakan Weaving Village

Experience authentic Yakan culture and witness traditional weaving techniques. Purchase beautiful handwoven textiles and crafts.

05

Merloquet Falls

Seven-tiered waterfall surrounded by lush forest. A hidden gem perfect for nature trekking and photography enthusiasts.

06

Zamboanga City Hall

Iconic landmark with stunning Spanish-inspired architecture. A must-visit for history buffs and architecture enthusiasts.

07

Vinta Cruise

Sail on traditional colorful vinta boats across Zamboanga's pristine waters. Experience the iconic symbol of the city.

08

Barter Trade Center

Unique shopping experience featuring goods from neighboring countries. Find exotic items, textiles, and local delicacies.

09

Canelar Barter Trade

Taste authentic Zamboanga cuisine including curacha, satti, and lokot-lokot. A paradise for food lovers and culinary explorers.
